Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 July 2026

1. Information We Collect

ToolSphere does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All tools run locally in your browser. No data is sent to our servers.

2. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for theme preference (dark/light mode) and tool usage history. These are stored locally in your browser and are not shared with any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

Some tools use external APIs (Gemini, Hugging Face, Remove.bg). These services have their own privacy policies. We do not store any data sent to these APIs.

4. Data Security

All data processing happens locally in your browser. We do not have access to your files or information.

5. Children's Privacy

ToolSphere is safe for all ages. We do not collect any personal information from children.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions, please contact us.
